Privacy & compliance

Built responsibly. Documented verifiably.

MOM Shield is not just a marketing promise — the architecture is built so that "data stays local" cannot work any other way technically.

🇩🇪 Developed in Germany

MOM Shield is developed in Germany. No US-corporation data flows, no hidden cloud sync, no third-party telemetry.

🔒 Data does not leave the device

Masking happens in your browser or your desktop app. Original data is never sent to a MOM Shield server — there simply isn't one.

📋 Audit log with CSV export

Every masking, unmasking, and file access is recorded in the local DB with timestamp, user, and rule version. Exportable as CSV at the press of a button — for audits, DPOs, and management.

🔁 Pseudonymization, not marketing speak

We deliberately speak of pseudonymization, not anonymization. The original values are recoverable — on your device, with your token. That matches the GDPR definition and is technically accurate.

🛡️ Signed license

Every commercial license is signed with an Ed25519 key pair. Tamper-resistant, verifiable, checkable offline. Suitable for ISMS-certified environments.

👤 Role model from v1.2

Admin and standard user are separated per installation. Self-registration can be disabled, a maximum user limit can be set. Password reset by the admin on your own server.

Frequently asked questions

What you're probably asking right now.

Is my data sent to MOM Shield?

No. There is no central MOM Shield server that could receive your data. Masking runs entirely on your device — in the browser or the desktop app. Even updates and license checks happen offline; only the updater manifest and installer downloads come from the public GitHub repository.

What does the AI actually see?

Structured placeholders like [[PERSON_001]], [[IBAN_001]], or [[ACCOUNT_001]]. The AI understands the grammar and can work with the placeholders — it just doesn't know who's behind them. When the response contains the same placeholders, MOM Shield replaces them on your device with the original values.

Is this GDPR-compliant?

MOM Shield is a pseudonymization tool within the meaning of Art. 4(5) GDPR. Processing by the AI provider happens exclusively with pseudonymized data — that significantly reduces the risk compared to unencrypted transmission. A full legal assessment must still be made by your DPO in the context of your specific processing.

How many employees can use the desktop app?

Up to 10 users per installation, each with their own account. One admin per installation. Larger setups (multiple locations, central policies) are on the roadmap — talk to us for a pilot.

What does MOM Shield cost?

The Chrome extension is free. The desktop app is currently in pilot phase — licenses are issued individually and are discounted or free during the pilot, depending on agreement. Regular pricing follows with general market launch.

Does this also work with Microsoft Copilot or local models?

The Chrome extension currently covers ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot (Microsoft), Gemini, and Grok. The desktop app is AI-agnostic — you mask locally and paste the masked text wherever you want. Local models (Ollama, LM Studio) work the same way.

How secure is the license file?

Every license is signed with an Ed25519 key pair. Any tampering with the contained data (expiry, seats, plan) is immediately detected and rejected by the app. The public key is built into every app version; the private key stays exclusively with us.

Can I deploy MOM Shield in a strictly regulated environment?

For ISMS-certified or regulated environments (e.g., supervised insurers, hospitals, public authorities) talk to us about an enterprise setup: on-premises variant, custom rule set, signed builds. It's part of our roadmap and is being built out together with first enterprise customers.
